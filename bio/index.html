Allan A. Martell (he/him) is a sixth-year PhD candidate at the School of Information at the University of Michigan and an alumni of the Graduate Certificate Program in Museum Studies. His work aims to leverage design as a means to promote reconciliation and justice in post-conflict contexts. Born and raised in El Salvador, he holds a B.A. in Social Communications from the Central American University, in El Salvador, and an M.S. in Digital Media from the Georgia Institute of Technology, in the United States.

Allan A. Martell, Ph.D. (he/him) is a postdoctoral researcher at the School of Library and Information Science at Louisiana State University where he collaborates with the Virtual Footlocker Project. His work aims to develop a design framework to study the collective memories of historical traumas, such as dictatorships, genocides, or civil wars. At stake in this research project is the opportunity to redress victims and promote redistributive justice in post-conflict societies.

Born and raised in El Salvador, Martell holds a B.A. in Social Communications from the Central American University, in El Salvador, and an M.S. in Digital Media from the Georgia Institute of Technology, in the United States.

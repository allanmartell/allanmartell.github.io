Allan A. Martell (he/him) is a postdoctoral researcher at the School of Library and Information Science at Louisiana State University, where he collaborates with the Virtual Footlocker Project. His work aims to leverage design as a means to promote reconciliation and justice in post-conflict contexts. Born and raised in El Salvador, he holds a B.A. in Social Communications from the Central American University, in El Salvador, and an M.S. in Digital Media from the Georgia Institute of Technology, in the United States.
